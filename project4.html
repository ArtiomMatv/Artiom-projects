Text-Based Adventure Game

Overview

A feature-rich Python text-based adventure game that offers an immersive experience through intricate levels, a compelling backstory, and interactive in-game mechanics. Players explore mysterious realms filled with hidden chests, valuable items, challenging NPCs, fierce monsters, and strategic trading.

Key Achievements

  • Developed a multi-level structure with escalating challenges and engaging narrative arcs.
  • Implemented in-game interactions including chests, potions, NPC dialogues, and boss battles.
  • Integrated a trading system and in-game currency to enhance strategic gameplay.
  • Built comprehensive test suites with pytest to ensure robust performance and debugging support.

Technologies Used:

Python, random, Streamlit, pydantic,pandas,numpy, pytest
